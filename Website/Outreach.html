Meeting with Primetime Robotics:

We met the Primetime robotics team, they are a 3rd year team and we had a meeting with them where we asked them to show us how to use Git. We learned that we can use either Bitbucket or Github. We asked a bunch of questions about the engineering notebook and what happens at the competition. We also asked a few questions on the website, for example we were wondering how they made their website. We asked how many controllers they used, and what way of code did they do. They taught us about things to know about FTC and gave us tips. While we taught them how to use CAD, a 3-D modeling app.
