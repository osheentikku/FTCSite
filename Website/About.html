About Us:

We are Hexspeak, a FIRST Tech Challenge team from Redmond, WA. Our team consists of 8 people, 2 boys and 6 girls. We are a rookie team who started FTC this year and are still learning lots of things. We've attended a few outreach events and even hosted a few of our own(More information about this can be found on the outreach page)! We are excited to design, build, and program our robot and can't wait to meet other teams!
