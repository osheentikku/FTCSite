Welcome!

We are a Hexspeak, a FIRSTÂ® Tech Challenge team from the Lake Washington School District. The FIRST Tech Challenge is a neighborhood (not utomatically district sponsored) program for students, grades 7-12, to gain knowledge about science, technology, engineering, and mathematics; or STEM through hands-on learning of robotics. Through the FIRST Tech Challenge program, our robotics team members build and program a robot. These robots will then compete at regional competitions, with the potential of qualifying for the Washington State tournament. Participating in this challenge allows our team to learn more about math and science, while expanding our knowledge of careers involving computer programming, business, and engineering. As you may know, getting young people interested in these fields is very important, not only to our community, but also to our state and country. Since this program is volunteer based, community support is important.
